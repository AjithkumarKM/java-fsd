History

Three decades ago, a small sweet shop was started by Raja, in the metropolis of Chennai. This same shop has now flowered into multiple branches that dot the length and breadth of Chennai as well as its neighbouring cities. Our Sweets & Snacks is now a well-established brand in Southern India that continues to grow with a vision to capture the global market. The baton of this sweet legacy was passed on by Raja to his sons, Ram and Rahul who now act as the driving force.
